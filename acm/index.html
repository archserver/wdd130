Alyona Chase

The Author: Alyona Chase has a Bachelors in Theology from Ukrain Evangelical Theology Seminary graduating in 2007 and has been a Baptist preacher in the country of Ukraine until 2010 when she moved to the United States. She started perching and producing in 2017 and she continues to preach twice a week scripture of the Bible through Video and has written many sermons which are available on this site.

She is interested in people gaining salvation being prepared to meet God and changing their life towards God. She is a disciple of Christ
